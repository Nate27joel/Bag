The Atelier

Where Every
Stitch
Tells a Story

In the heart of Florence's Oltrarno district, time moves differently.

Our atelier has occupied the same ochre-walled building since 1987. The tools have barely changed. The hands that hold them have grown more certain with every passing decade. We take roughly 48 hours to complete a single bag — not because we cannot move faster, but because we refuse to.
